QR Scanner

The scanner page supports three ways to read QR data: camera scan, uploaded QR image and manual input. For best testing, upload a clear QR code image or use Chrome/Edge for camera detection.

1. Upload QR Image

This is the recommended testing option. Select a clear QR code picture and click scan image. The page now uses jsQR for stronger image decoding.

QR image preview will appear here.

2. Camera Scanner

Start the camera and hold a QR code clearly in front of it.

Uploaded image scan result will appear here.
Camera scanner result will appear here.

3. Manual QR Data Input

Use this option when browser camera or image decoding is not supported.

Manual analysis result will appear here.
